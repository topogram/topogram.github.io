Release notes

Features

  • Upload your own dataset : Upload raw data files and start to use Topogram. .CSV and .TXT formats are supported.

  • Discussion Dynamics Online or offline, conversations can not be fitted in a single graph. Topogram shows how users interacts words by analyzing semantics and circulation.

  • Location-based analysis : Projection on maps with timelines allows to see the geographical spread of the information. Maps can be printed, exported and shared easily
